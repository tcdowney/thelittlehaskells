The Little Haskells

by Tim Downey

Introduction

It is the distant future, the year 2014. After a period of immense turmoil, the UN has declared CYBER MARTIAL LAW and has decreed that all software must be written in APL. Cyber terrorists around the globe have been stealing the monads from innocents around the globe. It is a bleak time indeed.

Yet in this dark hour, a lone beacon of hope still shines: the Little Haskells. Under the mind blowing leadership of Daniel Freedman, the Little Haskells are America's last line of defense against this hacker threat.
